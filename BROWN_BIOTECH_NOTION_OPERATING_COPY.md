# Brown Biotech Notion Operating Copy

## HQ
**Brown Biotech HQ**

## Operating principle
One owner. One next step. One human review gate.

## Core pages
- **Team Roster** — roles, ownership, and coverage
- **Intake Requests** — raw requests routed by service lane
- **Funnel** — qualified leads, follow-up, and next action
- **Decision Log** — key decisions with rationale
- **Weekly Review** — wins, blockers, risks, next moves

## Routing language

- **Supporting lanes:** biostatx, genox-site
- **CTA:** Request a Brief

## Short operating paragraph
Brown Biotech HQ keeps the company aligned around a single intake path. Requests enter the HQ workflow, get routed to the right lane, and move forward with human review for any high-stakes decision.
